In the depeart of Information Technology at Kamaraj College of Engineering and Technology, 100% Placement is available. Our teachers train our students for the placement, and in the last year, in the IT department, 100% of students were placed in good companies!!!

Kamaraj College of Engineering and Technology, Virudhunagar has a Training Development and Placement Cell (TDP) that helps students to enhance their skills. The placement cell of Kamaraj College of Engineering and Technology serves as a common link between recruiters and students. The goal of the Kamaraj College of Engineering and Technology placement cell is to assist students in improving their skills in accordance with industry expectations. During their first year, the students are taught communicative English as well as soft skills. More emphasis is placed on conceptual understanding and programming during the third and final years. To produce deployable engineers, special training in Java, Python, R-programming, Data Science and Big Data Analytics is provided.
